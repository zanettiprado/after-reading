About Us:

''Welcome to our book club! We are a group of passionate readers who meet weekly to discuss books and connect with fellow book lovers. Our club is open to anyone who loves to read, and we're always excited to welcome new members.''

Our Mission:

''Our mission is to create a vibrant community of readers who come together to share our love of books. We meet weekly after reading one predetermined book, and aim to read 54 books per year. Our meetings are focused on discussing the book, sharing our thoughts and opinions, and connecting with one another over our shared passion for literature.
Through our book club, we hope to encourage reading as a lifelong pursuit, foster meaningful connections among our members, and broaden our understanding of the world through the books we read. We believe that books have the power to inspire, challenge, and transform us, and we're committed to exploring this potential together as a community.
Whether you're a longtime reader or just getting started, we invite you to join us on this journey. Let's discover new worlds and perspectives together, one book at a time.''

Our Goals

Our goals include promoting a love of literature in a positive and nurturing environment, encouraging critical thinking and deeper engagement with stories, increasing imagination and creativity, reducing the chance of cognitive diseases, meeting new people and sharing experiences of reading, choosing books that expose members to new ideas, genres, and writing styles, and cultivating a reading habit while encouraging members to read more regularly. These goals are encapsulated in the "Our Goals" section of our website, which also features an eye-catching icon of a bullseye to represent our commitment to achieving these objectives.

Hangouts, Location and Time

Once a week, we gather together to discuss the book of the week, which has been carefully selected in advance. During our meetings, we share our thoughts, experiences, and insights on the book, fostering lively discussions that encourage critical thinking and deeper engagement with literature. We also take the opportunity to vote on the next book we will read and discuss in the following week, ensuring that everyone has a say in what we read next. Our book club is a welcoming and inclusive community that values the power of literature to inspire, challenge, and enrich our lives.
