About Us:

''Welcome to our book club! We are a group of passionate readers who meet weekly to discuss books and connect with fellow book lovers. Our club is open to anyone who loves to read, and we're always excited to welcome new members.''

Our Mission:

''Our mission is to create a vibrant community of readers who come together to share our love of books. We meet weekly after reading one predetermined book, and aim to read 54 books per year. Our meetings are focused on discussing the book, sharing our thoughts and opinions, and connecting with one another over our shared passion for literature.
Through our book club, we hope to encourage reading as a lifelong pursuit, foster meaningful connections among our members, and broaden our understanding of the world through the books we read. We believe that books have the power to inspire, challenge, and transform us, and we're committed to exploring this potential together as a community.
Whether you're a longtime reader or just getting started, we invite you to join us on this journey. Let's discover new worlds and perspectives together, one book at a time.''

Our Goals
  • Promoting love of literature in a positive and nurturing environment
  • Encouraging critical thinking and deeper engagement with stories
  • Increase your imagination and creativity
  • Reducing chance of cognitive diseases
  • Meet new people and share experiences of reading
